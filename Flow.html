Flow

For this project, we were tasked to create both iOS and Android versions of an app. With that in mind, I created a social calendar app called “Flow”. It is an app for users to create and share events on their calendars.

Objectives and Goals

  • Allow users to plan and schedule events.
  • Connect users with each other. Users will be able to add friends and create/collaborate on events together.

User Needs and Features/Functions

Identifying the user needs was an important part of the process. It helped set the foundation of what features and functions I wanted to include in my app.

The features and functions are created to give my app character, while also solving user problems and needs.

Competitor Analysis

Researching competitors gave me inspiration on how I want my app to look and feel. Learning about the different features and functions of the competitors helped me understand what features I wanted in my app. Seeing the strengths of the competitors was a huge factor in creating the design and functions of my app.

I wanted to create an app that has the strengths of my competitors, while adding my own style to it. The layouts and organization of the competitors laid the foundation of my app. Looking at how they positioned their categories and tabs helped me prioritize which tabs/screens were important to users.

After the research, it was time to interview users and see what other solutions I can create on my app to meet the different needs.

User Personas

User personas are important in organizing users with similar needs and wants. The personas help guide the direction of the app.

Personas were also created through research and interviews addressing the user's goals and frustrations. Interviewing the users really guided me in what goals I wanted to achieve with my travel app.

Listening to the frustrations and problems users come across really helped me implement features/functions that would address those problems.

User Flow

User flows help users understand the navigation of the app, and what users can expect when using the app. It is a diagram that allows users to visualize how to use the app, and how to achieve their needs/goals.

User flows helped organize the different components of the app - especially with the different tabs and pages that encompasses the app.

Creating the user flows was a vital part in laying the foundation for the framework of the user interface.
